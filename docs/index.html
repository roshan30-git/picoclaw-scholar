What It Does.

PDF Indexing Engine

Vector-based ingestion of syllabus documents. Converts unstructured PDF data and handwritten OCR into open-scale embeddings instantly.

Daily Quiz Generator

Automated spaced repetition system (SRS). Generates dynamic quiz questions pushed to WhatsApp based on recent weak points in your chat history.

Context Memory

Long-term persistence layer via SQLite. The model chronologically "remembers" your self-reflections and adapts its reasoning difficulty curve.

Exam Scheduler

Cron-like scheduling pipeline for your exam preparation. Integrates natively with Google Classroom to scrape and track upcoming deadlines.

Mermaid Diagrams

Text-to-Diagram rendering. Request complex flowcharts or electrical engineering architecture diagrams directly via Telegram WebApp overlays.

PYQ Predictor

Statistical frequency analysis of Previous Year Questions to mathematically predict and rank high-probability conceptual topics for upcoming finals.
